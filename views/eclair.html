Eclair's Diary

First Entry

Looking back, we thought it was only a game. A great adventure, so full of excitement, that we didn't care about the consequences. It was us, our partners, and we didn't need anything else.

We were Happy.

But we forgot that, even though it was a game for us, it was something important for others.

We cried, we suffered, we hated and we loved.

I guess, some of us would change things if we could. But that would make the sacrifices we made... useless.

If we could do it all over again, we would.

Let me tell you about our great adventure.
